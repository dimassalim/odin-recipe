Chicken Stuff

A delightful, well done, stuff, "still have no idea why there is chicken in the name :/"

Ingredient

  • 1 Pan
  • 2 Liters Oil
  • 1 Kg Chicken
  • A man
  • A wife if you have
  • 1 Bowl of Rice

Instruction

  1. Heat up the pan
  2. Heat up the man
  3. Add wife
  4. Beat the chicken
  5. Eat the rice
  6. Oil the pan man with heated wife
  7. Repeat till man is well done
